90g Unsalted butter
15g Powdered sugar
a pinch of Salt
18g Egg yolk
2g Vanilla extract
115g Plain flour
10g Almond powder
15g Corn starch

beat butter, add sugar, yolk, vanilla, flour, mis and refrigerate for an hour or so

300g Pineapple
35g Sugar
a pinch of Salt
35g Honey

boil pipeapple until it is a jam

make rounds from the dough, press into a little form or a ramekin, add the pineapple jam into the cavity and close it
turn on baking sheet, bake in 350 F for 10 min until slightly golden
