Recipe: 9 inch pan
1 cup = 250 ml

Sweet Shortcrust Pastry:
1 1/3 cup (240 g) all-purpose flour
½ tsp baking powder
120 g butter, softened, room t
¼ cup (60 g) sugar
pinch of salt
1 medium egg
refrigerate the dough for 15-20 minutes, then spread Frangipane and put apricots and raspberries on top.

Frangipane:
~ ½ cup (60 g ) almonds (toasted in oven) or 60 g ground almonds (flour)
 ¼ cup (60 g) sugar
60 g butter, room t
1 medium egg
1 tsp vanilla extract

Topping:
~ 400 g apricots
½ cup fresh or frozen raspberries
2 tbsp almond flakes

190° C / 360 F 40-45 minutes until golden brown
