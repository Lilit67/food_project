CARAMEL COOKIES
(quick and easy)
Ingredients:
•  125g butter softened
• 1/2 cup brown sugar
• 2 tbs golden syrup (or maple syrup)
• 1 cup self-raising !our
Instructions:
• Preheat oven to 180ºc.
• Cream butter and sugar.
• Add syrup and beat until fluffy.
• Mix in !our until texture is suitable to roll into balls.
• Place on a paper lined baking tray, allowing room for biscuits to spread. Press biscuits gently with a fork.
• Bake at 180C for 15 minutes or until golden.
